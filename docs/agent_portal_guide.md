# Agent Portal User Guide

Welcome to the new NestFind Agent Portal. This guide introduces the advanced features designed to help you manage your territory, leads, and earnings effectively.

## 1. Dashboard 2.0
The new dashboard is your command center.

- **Real-time Stats**: Track earnings, active deals, and new requests instantly.
- **AI Insights**: The right sidebar provides actionable intelligence, such as price drop recommendations or verification reminders, powered by our predictive algorithms.
- **Earnings Chart**: Visualize your income trends over time.

## 2. CRM & Lead Management
Navigate to the **CRM** tab to manage your relationships.

- **Lead Cards**: View potential buyers (from visits) and sellers (from assignments) in one place.
- **Status Tracking**: Monitor lead stages from 'New' to 'Closed'.
- **Quick Actions**: Call or email leads directly from the card.

## 3. Analytics & Performance
The **Analytics** tab offers a deep dive into your business metrics.

- **Revenue Breakdown**: See where your earnings are coming from.
- **Activity Metrics**: Track your weekly visits and calls.
- **Commission Calculator**: Quickly estimate potential earnings for new deals based on property value and commission rate.
- **Goal Tracking**: Set and monitor monthly revenue and closure goals.

## 4. Assignment Workflow
The core assignment flow remains streamlined:

1.  **Receive Request**: New assignments appear in your dashboard.
2.  **Accept/Decline**: Review property details before accepting.
3.  **Verify**: Use the mobile-friendly verification flow to validate property details and photos.
4.  **Close**: track the deal through to completion.

## 5. Support
If you encounter any issues or need help with the new features, contact the support team or check the system design docs for technical details.
